About Autoxai

We Automate So You Can Grow

Built by a team obsessed with making AI practical and affordable for every Indian business.

Autoxai was born from a simple frustration: Indian businesses were losing money and time on tasks that AI could handle in seconds. Owners spending hours following up leads manually. Restaurants taking orders over the phone. Clinics missing appointments because nobody sent reminders.

We set out to fix that — building practical AI automation that plugs directly into how Indian businesses actually work. WhatsApp-first, affordable, and fast to deploy.

Today, we help businesses across India automate their lead generation, customer communication, ordering systems, CRM management, and more — so they can focus on the work that actually requires a human.

Our mission is simple: give every Indian business access to the same AI tools that big companies use — at a price that makes sense.
